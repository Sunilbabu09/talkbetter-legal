# Privacy Policy - TalkBetter

**Effective Date:** February 16, 2026

TalkBetter ("we", "our", or "us") operates the TalkBetter mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## 1. Information We Collect

TalkBetter does not require account registration.

When you use the app:

- The text you enter is temporarily sent to our secure backend servers.
- The text is processed by an AI service to generate reply suggestions.
- We do not permanently store your messages.

We may collect:

- Anonymous usage analytics (if enabled)
- App crash reports
- Device type and app version (for debugging)

## 2. How We Use Information

We use your text input only to:

- Generate AI-powered reply suggestions
- Improve system reliability
- Detect abuse or misuse

We do not sell your data.  
We do not use your conversations for advertising.

## 3. Data Storage

- User text is processed in real-time.
- We do not store conversations permanently.
- We do not build user profiles.

## 4. Third-Party Services

TalkBetter uses:

- Firebase (Google) for backend hosting
- AI processing services to generate responses

These services may temporarily process your input to generate replies.

## 5. Children's Privacy

TalkBetter is not intended for children under 13.

## 6. Data Security

We use secure HTTPS connections to transmit data.  
All backend services follow industry-standard security practices.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time.  
Updates will be reflected with a new effective date.
